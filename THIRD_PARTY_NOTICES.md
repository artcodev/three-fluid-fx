## Additional References

The following references informed the implementation and documentation, but no
third-party source files from them are vendored in this package:

- Jos Stam, _Stable Fluids_ (SIGGRAPH 1999)
- Fedkiw, Stam, Jensen, _Visual Simulation of Smoke_ (SIGGRAPH 2001)
- Kim, Liu, Llamas, Rossignac, _Advections with Significantly Reduced
  Dissipation and Diffusion_ (2007)
- mofu-dev, _Stable Fluids with three.js_
- mnmxmx/fluid-three
